About Us
We are the Genesee Early College Esports Team. We strive to build each other up and work on social skills while also having fun playing competitively. We have seperate teams for Rocket League, lead by Cobra, and Overwatch, lead by HinduNosan. During the year, we meet in the ITS Lab every Monday, Thursday, and Friday after school. However, when Spring Classes start, these meetings will cease to exist. If you are a GEC student and would like to join, please sign up here.
