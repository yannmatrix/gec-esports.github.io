Welcome!
This is the Genesee Early College Esports Team. We strive to build each other up and work on social skills while also having fun playing competitively. If you are a GEC student and would like to join, please sign up here.
State of the Art Esports Lab
Here at Genessee Early College, we partnered with the University of Michigan-Flint to provide a state of the art Esports Lab from the Head of ITS. Equipped with 3080ti Alienware computers and 244hz monitors, the team is best equipped with tools to play and win hard.
